Activation Guide

Start Your Journey With Us

Here are the quick steps to easily monitor your EC7 Powerbank:

Step 1: Begin by reading the instructions carefully on the packaging. Then, scan the QR code located on the powerbank’s digital screen, which will take you to the EC7 GPS Tracker website. Once scanned, your EC7 Powerbank will automatically link to your account.

Step 2: Register by entering your personal details and log into your account.

Step 3: Navigate the map located on the right side of the website to view the real-time location of your EC7 powerbank.
